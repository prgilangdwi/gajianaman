## 3. ARCHITECTURE NOTES

### Decision: Governance-First Approach
Instead of jumping directly to component changes, Phase 01 Batch 1 established the governance skeleton. This ensures all downstream sessions operate under the same rules, preventing context bloat and ensuring deterministic execution.

### Configuration Structure
The `.claude/settings.json` file serves as the machine-readable enforcement layer for governance rules, while `CLAUDE.md` serves as the human-readable reference. Both are synchronized and must be updated together.

### Single Source of Truth
All governance now references `master-development-roadmap.md` as the ultimate authority. This prevents conflicting guidance and simplifies decision-making in future sessions.
